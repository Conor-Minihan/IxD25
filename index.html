Introduction

Welcome to the introduction of my interaction design project, focused on gamifying a learning app. Our aim is to make the learning experience more engaging and interactive for our users.

Gamification involves applying game design principles to non-game contexts such as education. The objective is to increase user motivation, engagment and enjoyment.

The project involves various phases, including a competitive analysis, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. The competitive analysis will help me identify strengths and weaknesses in the existing market, and highlight areas we can improve

Wireframing and prototyping allowed us to design and develop the interface and user experience of the app, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable learning process.

Finally, useability testing was conducted to gather feedback from users on the apps usability and functionality. The feedback will be used to refine and improve the app, ensuring that the users needs are met.

Overall, our aim is to create a learning app that is not only effective but also enjoyable, ultimatley improving the users learning outcomes.
